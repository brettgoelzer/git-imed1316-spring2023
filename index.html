What is FunkyFungi?

FunkyFungi is a website dedicated to spreading information about fungi to people across the globe. By having information on the science, growing, and edibility on mushrooms and other fungus, we have all this infromation free to acess for anyone.

The science behind fungi is very intricate. There are always new discoveries coming out everyday. Only the surface has been touched in Mycology and by providing information and discussions, we encourage you to experiemnt safely and learn about the lives of fungi.

Growing mushrooms and cooking with mushrooms may seem challenging and confusing, but it's actually quite simple and can be done by anyone almost anywhere! FunkyFungi provides information and products to help you learn how to grow, cultivate, and use mushrooms.

With a discussion form, anyone can access these forms and see what people have to say and have discovered. By becoming a member of FunkyFungi (it's free!), you get access to posting on the forms and gain many more benefits. More info about becoming a member on the member registration form.
